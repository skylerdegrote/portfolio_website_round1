Mary Kay

Mary Kay was the client for the National Student Advertising Competition 2014. We were given a task to create an entire campaign to give Mary Kay a new look and develop interest for the brand for ages 18 to 24 men and women. For this project, the entire class was given the same project and we were split into different groups. In the district competition, located at the U of M in Minneapolis, MN, we placed 3rd.

My role during this project was a designer. I created advertisements and illustrations that we used in the campaign. I also developed some pieces that we ended up not using in the final project.

I used Adobe Illustrator CC and Adobe Photoshop CC for the pieces in this project

The Soul collection

The Soul Collection is a series of three books, which is unfinished at the moment. I have written both of the books that are out and I also designed the covers, and wrote the summaries on the back. The series is about a set of triplets that have been reborn in each generation and have the ongoing duty of saving the world from evil.

Book 1: Soul

The thirteenth generation of demon hunters and yet they may be burdened with the largest responsibility since the very first generation. Their family history has been filled with ancestors and stories of the infamous triplets that bring Leadership, Strength, and Witchcraft together to keep the world from becoming ‘Hell on Earth’. Up until now, only the very first generation had to face the ultimate demon and their way of handling it imprisoned him in the dimension of Hell in the 1800’s. Now, in 2011, the current triplets find themselves needing to either prevent his escape or kill him once and for all. Will they find the strength, knowledge, and courage to save the world as their ancestors did so long ago?

Book 2: The Guardian of the Souls

Julie has disappeared and both Sadie and Kate need to find her, but where to start and how to find someone that just vanished right in front of their eyes is unclear. After destroying the devil and publicly announcing the existence of demons, vampires, and witches, the Sanders Triplets find themselves now split up. Was she kidnapped or did she go willingly? Day by Day, Sadie finds herself gaining the powers of a witch. For 13 generations there has always only been one witch amongst the triplets, why now does this generation have two? Does this mean that Julie is dead?
