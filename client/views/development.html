Rocket Resource

The rocket resource application was my personal project at Prime Digital Academy. I chose this subject because as a kid I loved going out with family and launching rockets for fun. This application is directed towards kids in the way of getting them excited about making their own rocket unique.

My plan for this application will be to add some functionality. I am going to make the page printable so kids can print their customized rocket and color it. I am going to add a store functionality to make it so you can see what pieces you would need to make your customized rocket for real and where you might be able to purchase those pieces.
